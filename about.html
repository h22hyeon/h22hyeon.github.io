About Me

Learn more about my background, research interests, and journey

Background

I'm a Ph.D. student in the School of Computing at KAIST and a member of the Big Data Intelligence Lab, advised by Professor Joyce Jiyoung Whang.

My research interests include graph ML, trustworthy and explainable AI for real-world deployment. I'm passionate about developing AI systems that are not only powerful but also reliable, interpretable, and safe for real-world applications.

Research Interests

My work spans multiple domains, focusing on making AI systems more robust and trustworthy. I'm particularly interested in graph neural networks for fraud detection and understanding vulnerabilities in large language models.

Feel free to reach out for collaborations or research discussions. I'm always open to discussing new ideas and potential collaborations.
